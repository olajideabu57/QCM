General FAQs

Your Questions Answered

  • What types of medical emergencies do you handle?

    We handle a wide range of emergencies, including trauma, cardiac events, respiratory distress, and critical care transfers, with fully equipped ambulances.

  • What equipment do your ambulances carry?

    Our ambulances are equipped with advanced life support systems, including ventilators, defibrillators, oxygen supplies, and monitoring devices for comprehensive care.

  • Do you provide inter-facility transfers?

    Yes, we offer inter-facility transfers for patients requiring transport between hospitals or medical centers, with both secure and non-secure options.

  • Are your services covered by insurance?

    Many of our services may be covered by insurance, depending on the provider and policy. Contact us for assistance with insurance verification.
