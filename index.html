Secure Patient Transport

We provide specialist transport for individuals who may pose a risk to themselves or others due to mental health conditions or behavioural challenges. Our team is trained in de-escalation and patient handling techniques, ensuring every journey is conducted with respect, care, and discretion.

  • Staff trained in mental health and challenging behaviour management
  • Discreet, confidential transport process
  • Risk assessments conducted before each journey
  • CQC-compliant service delivery
  • 24/7 availability for urgent transfers

Secure Vehicle Transfers

Our secure vehicles are fitted with specialized safety features to support the safe transport of high-risk or vulnerable patients. Each vehicle is maintained to the highest standard and designed to ensure the comfort and safety of both patients and staff during transit.

  • Equipped with secure compartments and reinforced barriers
  • Real-time GPS tracking for added safety
  • Built-in communication systems for team coordination
  • Regularly cleaned and sanitized to clinical standards
  • Designed for safe restraint and smooth transport

Patient Transport Service (PTS)

Our non-emergency Patient Transport Service (PTS) is designed to get patients to and from hospital, clinic, and care appointments safely and comfortably. Whether it's a one-off trip or a regular arrangement, we tailor transport to each individual's mobility and support needs.

  • Reliable, on-time pickups and drop-offs
  • Friendly, professional staff with enhanced DBS checks
  • Comfortable, clean, and climate-controlled vehicles
  • Support for elderly or vulnerable individuals
  • Booking available for one-off or recurring appointments

Court Escort Service

  • Detailed travel planning and communication

General FAQs

Your Questions Answered

  • What types of clients do you transport?

    We provide secure, non-emergency transport for individuals with mental health conditions, behavioural challenges, or vulnerabilities that require specialist handling. Our services are used by local authorities, hospitals, care providers, and legal institutions.

  • What equipment do your ambulances carry?

    Our ambulances are equipped with advanced life support systems, including ventilators, defibrillators, oxygen supplies, and monitoring devices for comprehensive care.

  • Do you provide inter-facility transfers?

    Yes, we offer inter-facility transfers for patients requiring transport between hospitals or medical centers, with both secure and non-secure options.

  • Are your staff trained to manage challenging behaviour?

    Yes. Our team is fully trained in managing challenging behaviour, de-escalation techniques, and safeguarding protocols. We ensure each journey is handled with professionalism, patience, and respect.
